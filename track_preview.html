    Just Race Exclusive Race Way

    Our track is a 1.2 mile, 9 turn road course located in the center of the Just Race speed way. The average cornering speed for this course ranges from 80 kph on the first left handed corner to 135 kph. The Ferrari F430 GT can reach speeds up to 220 kph / 135 mph in the straight away and lateral G-Forces up to 1.6. This challenging track is the perfect location to race on. It offers visibility of every upcoming portion of the track allowing you to improve on every lap.

    Corner Breifing

    • TURN 1 2 3: Is a very fast chicane where you will use the F1 style curbing before heading onto a short straight.
    • TURN 4 5: A tight, left/right chicane with very heavy break zone heading on to the back straight.
    • TURN 6: Wide left progressively getting tighter leading into the hairpin
    • TURN 7 8: Is a VERY technical section with the right hairpin with a kinked left on the exit
    • TURN 9: Is a 3rd gear fast and challenging one with a slight uphill and decreasing radius leading into the final corner before the straight.
    • TURN 10: The final left uphill leading into the main straight.
